About me

A small introduction about my self

Luke Woollett

Software Engineer from Christchurch, New Zealand

Hi! My name is Luke Woollett, and i'm a Software Engineer, Currently working at JADE Software Corportation.
I'd wanted to do software ever since I was a kid, but started with the want to do game development. I started this with making minecraft server plugins in Java.
When I went to High School, I started going to an after School Program for High Schoolers at the University Of Canterbury where you learned Python. I started making some games in pygame, and generally enjoyed my time there.
In high school, my teacher put me and a friend of mine forward to do the STAR programme, which put High School students into first year University papers. After high school, I went to University and eventually got my degree in Computer Science
Outside of programming, I have a lot of hobbies, some of which are football, working on motorbikes, 3D printing and house reno.
You'll notice a fair few photos of my motorbikes scattered through this site.
